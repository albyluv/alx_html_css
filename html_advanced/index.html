FAQ

How much does it cost to attend Smile School?

It costs nothing, Smile School is a non-profit school financed by the community.

What kind of people attend Smile School?

Our students have extremely diverse backgrounds. Some of them are fresh graduates, others are looking to start a new career.

How competitive is admission?

Admission is completely merit based, we only look at your coding skills, not your background.

How much time do I need to dedicate?

Smile School is an intensive program. You will need to dedicate at least 2 months of your life to it.
